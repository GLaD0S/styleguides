Color

Each festival track is assigned unique background and border colors each year. In the settings Sass file, there are two variables for each track, one for background color and one for border color. This is for ease of switching out track colors, as well as using these colors in new places. Track colors are listed below in alphabetical order by variable.

$criminal_justice_border
#F7922C
$criminal_justice_bg
rgba(247, 146, 44, 0.5)
$criminal_justice_bg
rgba(247, 146, 44, 0.5)
